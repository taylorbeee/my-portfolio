Get to know me!

Hey there! I'm Taylor Behrens and I'm a Frontend Web Developer who loves to bring digital projects to life. I have always loved soving puzzles and chasing the dopamine rush of finding the answer to a tough problem.

In University I began my journey in development with a few intro classes to HTML, CSS and Java. Soon enough I found myself at a crossroads - do I continue on this route to a computer science profession? Or take the far more stable career path of an actor/writer? Hard-cut to me now, where I have rediscovered my love for coding and problem-solviing. My entertainment background is now my secret weapon as it has trained me to think out-of-the-box and find new, creative ways to resolve issues, communicate ideas and work with expansive, diverse teams.

When I'm not coding you can find me, listening to film podcasts, reading sci-fi, or bringing up the rear at my local running club.
